RULEThree things to never get wrong

  • Undercurrents are bands, not boxes. Quality, governance, orchestration, cost touch every stage. Drawing them in the flow is the junior tell.
  • Migration is two jobs. Moving data is easy; proving it arrived intact (reconciliation) is the hard, billable one.
  • The grain is sacred. Declare it at Transform — "one row per ___" — before anything else, or every downstream number lies.
